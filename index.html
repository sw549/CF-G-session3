About:

UP-SIGN (UK-Pakistan Science and Innovation Global Network) was set up to raise awareness of the specific development problems of Pakistan and to bridge the gap between Pakistan and UK researchers, universities, organisations and business sector. The Network will also address global challenges through discipline specific research themes and act as a hub to support cooperative research e.g. GCRF (Global Challenges Research Fund) and related overseas development funding which includes Pakistan.
